About Jae-Hoon

Jae-Hoon is a rising star in the K-pop world, known for his powerful vocals and charismatic stage presence. As the main vocalist of the popular group "Stardust," Jae-Hoon has captured the hearts of fans worldwide with his soulful performances and genuine personality.

With his unique blend of pop and R&B influences, Jae-Hoon is redefining the K-pop sound and pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the industry.

Fun Facts

  • Birthday: January 15, 1998
  • Height: 180 cm
  • Position: Main Vocalist
  • Hobbies: Composing, Painting
